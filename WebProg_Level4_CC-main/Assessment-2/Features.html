KEY FEATURES

DEFYING GAMEPLAY

The Stanley Parable is often described as a "walking simulator," but it’s really more like a philosophical experiment wearing the disguise of a video game.

Core Gameplay Elements

Exploration
You explore an abandoned office building and its surreal hidden areas.
Choice
Every decision, big or small, can lead to a different outcome.
Narration
The omniscient narrator comments, questions, or criticizes your actions.
Endings
Over a dozen unique endings await. Some are serious, others satirical or nonsensical.

Unique Features

Self-Aware
The game talks to you—not just your character.
Meta-Humor
It parodies game design, player expectations, and narrative logic.
Replay Focus
The game changes with each run, encouraging experimentation.
It’s not a challenge to win, but a story to explore. Or perhaps to dismantle.
