STORY OVERVIEW

STANLEY'S STORY

Stanley is just a man. He sits in an office. He presses buttons on a monitor as instructed. Day after day. Life is simple—until one morning, when the instructions stop. Everyone in the building is gone.

Suddenly alone, Stanley steps out of his office and begins exploring. But he’s not really alone—a narrator guides him, describing every move Stanley should make. You can obey… or not.

Each time you play, your choices lead to different paths and different endings—some funny, some eerie, some deeply philosophical. The story can reset, change tone, break the fourth wall, or even collapse into absurdity.

“Stanley walked through the door on his left…”
But what if you walk through the one on the right?

The Stanley Parable isn’t about reaching an ending—it’s about discovering how every decision shapes a new story.
