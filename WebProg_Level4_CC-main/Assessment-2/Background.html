BACKGROUND DEVELOPMENT

BEHIND THE RELEASE

The Stanley Parable began in 2011 as a Half-Life 2 mod created by Davey Wreden, a college student interested in narrative experimentation. It quickly gained a cult following for its originality.

In 2013, Wreden teamed up with level designer William Pugh to create a full remake using the Source engine. This version featured:

  • ✔ New content and endings
  • ✔ Improved environments
  • ✔ Professional narration by Kevan Brighting
  • ✔ Enhanced story complexity

Ultra Deluxe Edition (2022)

In 2022, The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe was released with:

  • Console support (PS, Xbox, Switch)
  • A completely new “New Content” route with additional endings
  • Self-referential commentary on remakes and the gaming industry itself

The expanded game blurred the line between sequel and remake, taking meta-commentary to a new level.

The game doesn’t just ask what games can be—it asks what itself is.
