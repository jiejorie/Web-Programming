THE PARABLE BEGINS

“This is the story of a man named Stanley.”

Welcome to your one-stop destination for exploring the strange, hilarious, and thought-provoking world of The Stanley Parable. This critically acclaimed narrative game asks a simple question with complex implications:

What happens when you refuse to play by the rules?

This website is designed to help you explore The Stanley Parable in greater depth. Whether you're new to the game or revisiting it after years, you’ll find:

  • ✔ A plot overview that avoids spoilers
  • ✔ Detailed explanations of gameplay and core mechanics
  • ✔ A look at the game's unique development history
  • ✔ Critical reception and its impact on game design and storytelling

Whether you’re new to the game or returning for the 47th time, this is the place to discover why The Stanley Parable continues to captivate players and redefine what a game can be.
