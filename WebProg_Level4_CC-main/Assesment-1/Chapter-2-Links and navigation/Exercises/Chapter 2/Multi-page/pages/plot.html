Synopsis

Set in the Generation Loss universe, Generation Loss: The Social Experiments is a suspenseful yet comedic tale of horror and mystery presented by Showfall Media, a television broadcasting company, using interactive audience participation.

The story follows a 19-year-old teenager, known as 'The Hero,' who wakes up in a cold sweat inside a mysterious cabin without the slightest hint of recollection. As he attempts to escape, he encounters multiple personalities who challenge him to complete a series of deadly puzzles in exchange for freedom. Along the way, he forms alliances, witnesses tragic deaths, and fights for his survival in this eerie and unpredictable environment.

A thought-provoking narrative that challenges perspectives and perceptions of reality, Generation Loss: The Social Experiments explores themes of control, morality, and the consequences of a world obsessed with endless entertainment.
