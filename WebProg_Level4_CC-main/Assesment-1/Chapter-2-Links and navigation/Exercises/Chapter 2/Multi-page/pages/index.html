ABOUT

The Series

Generation Loss: The Social Experiments was a series of interactive horror-comedy livestreams under the Generation Loss franchise, created by content creator Ranboo. The series aired on the platform Twitch on the 24th to the 28th of May 2023, while the founder's cut premiered on June 15th of 2024. It features many other content creators playing the supporting roles within the series, along with narrative references to the film franchise Saw.

Additionally, in The Streamer Awards, it was nominated for the "Best Streamed Series", officially recognized as a standout in the world of digital entertainment, showcasing its excellence in storytelling, production quality, and audience engagement. This three-part series is the first of many generations to come within this franchise, so stay tuned!


The Creator

Ranboo, born on November 2, 2003, is an online content creator known for producing variety-style content with an emphasis on gaming across both Twitch and YouTube. He is celebrated for his unique and charismatic humor, as well as his distinctive persona, which includes his tall stature and masked face.

Additionally, Ranboo is an openly queer creator who uses he/they pronouns and has made significant contributions to charitable causes, reportedly raising over $100,000 for the Trevor Project in 2021. Combining these qualities, Ranboo has cultivated a loyal and dedicated fanbase, a testament to their impactful and valuable digital presence.
